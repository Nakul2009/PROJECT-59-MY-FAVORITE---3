Geronimo Stilton is an Italian children's book series written by Elisabetta Dami (though the covers claim they were written by the title character).[1] On older novels, the copyright is issued to a pseudonym "Geronimo Stilton"; this was changed on newer books. The books are intended for readers in the 8–12 year-old age range. The books are also intended for readers on the N level.

Scholastic Corporation has published the English version of the series in the US since February 2004, and the books are published by Sweet Cherry Publishing in the UK.

The series has sold over 180 million copies worldwide, and with 249 editions it is one of the best-selling book series ever written.[2] The books have been translated into 49 languages, with more translations to come. [3]

In the series, the title character is an anthropomorphic Dutch-American mouse who lives in the fictional New Mouse City (a probable play on New York City) on fictional Mouse Island. A best-selling author, Geronimo Stilton[4] works as a journalist and editor for the fictional newspaper The Rodent's Gazette.

He has a younger sister named Thea Stilton,[5] a cousin named Trap Stilton,[5] and a favourite nephew, nine-year-old Benjamin Stilton (Ben) and also Petunina Pretty Paws. Geronimo is a nervous, mild-mannered mouse who would like nothing better than to live a quiet life, but he keeps getting involved in faraway adventures with Thea, Trap, and Benjamin, and sometimes Aunt Sweetfur. The books are written as though they are autobiographical adventure stories.

Papercutz obtained the rights to publish English translations of the graphic novels of Geronimo Stilton. These retain some of the original names of the characters that were changed in the storybooks translated by Scholastic. For example, the English translations of the graphic novels by Papercutz continue to use the names "Patty Spring" and "Pandora", while the storybooks by Scholastic changed these names to Petunia Pretty Paws and Bugsy Wugsy, respectively.

Characters from Geronimo Stilton[edit]

The Stilton Family[edit]

The Stiltons are the main protagonists of the series. They worked at the newspaper company The Rodent's Gazette. They travel together, sometimes with their friends, and sometimes just Geronimo. They explore and solve mysteries.

  • Geronimo Stilton: The publisher of The Rodent's Gazette. He is portrayed as brave in the television series, but even though in the books he was never depicted himself as valiant. He wears glasses in the book series, but no glasses in the television series. Geronimo has a love of knowledge and has a sense of ethics and morals, but despite this, he is still a bit lonely. He leads one of the most successful publication groups in the world.
  • Thea Stilton: Geronimo's younger sister and the special correspondent for The Rodent's Gazette. For a time, Thea taught a journalism class at Whale Island University, and while there she met five mouselets. These mouselets, Pamela, Paulina, Violet, Colette, and Nicky named themselves the Thea sisters, after their favorite teacher, Thea Stilton. Thea loves to have an adventure with Trap, Benjamin, and Geronimo and exploring for an article in The Rodent's Gazette. Thea is always ready to jump into action. She also drives a motorcycle and a Helicopter which is portrayed to the book. While she is portrayed as brave and adventurous (and well prepared too), she is often reckless and often gets into trouble while searching for stories.
  • Trap Stilton: Geronimo's cousin. He travels along with Geronimo when he's called on. Trap is a joker and loves to play tricks on others, especially Geronimo who he considers to be gullible. Still, he has a big heart and does not play jokes to be mean. He is also easily frightened.
  • Benjamin Stilton: Geronimo and Thea's young nephew. Who loves Geronimo and often comes along with Geronimo on his many adventures.

Recurring characters[edit]

  • Hercule Poirat: Geronimo's childhood friend and private investigator of New Mouse City. He likes to play tricks, especially on Geronimo. His disguises are unpredictable. Hercule has a passion for bananas, even his vehicles are banana-shaped. He has a crush on Geronimo's sister, Thea. He also brings Geronimo along on many of his cases. His name is a play on Hercule Poirot. (During his sole appearance in graphic novel #3, The Coliseum Con, he is identified as "Nosymouse Squeak.")
  • Creepella von Cacklefur: She is an enchanting and mysterious mouse. She is a special effects designer for scary films and haunted houses. A journalist that lives in Mysterious Valley. She has a pet bat named Bitewing. She has a huge crush on Geronimo and wants to become his girlfriend. She is the protagonist of her own spin-off Geronimo Stilton book series.
  • Petunia Pretty Paws: niece of Bobby Paws and Suzy Rattella and twin sister of Wolfgang. She is a TV reporter. She dedicated her life to protecting nature and saving the environment. Geronimo has a crush on her.
  • Wolfgang Wild Paws: Son of Bobby Paws and Suzy Rattella and twin brother of Petunia. He is a TV producer. Like his sister, he too dedicated his life to protecting nature and saving the environment.
  • Bugsy Wugsy: Daughter of John Wugsy and Furry Paws and Benjamin's best friend. She wants to save the environment like her aunt Petunia.
  • Bruce Hyena: Geronimo's adventures friend. Bruce also like to sign Geronimo up on many crazy adventures that Geronimo did not want to go on. He also has a crush on Geronimo's sister,Thea,just like Hercule Poirat.
  • Shorty Tao: She is Bruce Hyena's cousin, the managing editor of The Rodent's Gazette, and a karate world champion. She has a little brother, Baby Tao.

The Stingysnout Family[edit]

The Stingysnouts are a cheap family who find a way to not spend a lot of money and reuse materials they have. Their relationship between the Stiltons and the Stingysnout begins when Samuel's great-grandfather married Geronimo's great-great-grandmother. Like the Stiltons they are good-hearted.

  • Samuel S. Stingysnout: The head of the Stingysnout family.
  • Chintzina Stilton (Nẽe Stingysnout): Samuel's younger sister
  • Stevie Stingysnout: Samuel's son.
  • Zelda Stingysnout: Samuel's cousin.
  • Thriftella and Worthington: Twins.
  • Grandma and Grandpa Cheaperly: Samuel's parents
  • Ivy and Hoarden Accounts: Ivy is Samuel's daughter and Hoarden is Ivy's husband.
  • Penniford and Saveanna: Children of Ivy and Hoarden.

The Paws Family[edit]

The family lived on a farm for many generations in the Giant Sequoia Valley, in Dolphin Bay. They are good friends with the Stiltons.

  • Grandfather Paws: Well-versed in natural sciences and once traveled the world in search of rare butterflies.
  • Grandmother Paws: At a young age, she traveled all over the world when planes were not built yet.
  • Teddy Paws: Son of Grandfather and Grandmother Paws and brother of Bobby Paws. He runs the farm with his brother.
  • Jenny Littlepaw: Teddy's wife. She's an exceptional cook.
  • Tom Paws: Son of Teddy and Jenny. He adores working with his father and inherited a passion for natural sciences from his grandfather.
  • Lucy Vally: Tom's wife. She has a passion for growing all kinds of plants, flowers, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Lilly, Lally, and Lolly Paws: Daughters of Lucy and Tom. Lilly is five and adores helping her mother in the garden. Lally is seven, she enjoys horseback riding. Lolly is the eleven and dreams of traveling like Grandmother Paws.
  • Bobby Paws: Son of Grandfather and Grandmother Paws and brother of Teddy Paws. He also worked on the farm.
  • Suzy Rattella: Bobby's wife. She has a passion for dolphins and works for the Marine Center in New Mouse City.
  • Furry Paws: Daughter of Bobby and Suzy and sister of Petunia and Wolfgang.
  • John Wugsy: Furry's husband. He loves living in the country and taking long bike rides.
  • Bugsy Wugsy: daughter of Furry and John.

Character from Thea Stilton/Mouseford Academy[edit]

Thea Sisters[edit]

The Thea Sisters are five mice and protagonists of Geronimo's spin-off series Thea Stilton book series. They named their group after Thea Stilton who sometimes teaches journalism at Mouseford Academy. The Thea Sisters have adventures around the world or Mouseford Academy.

  • Nicky: She is from Australia. She loves open spaces and nature. Nicky has a positive attitude. She does not like small, tight places or sitting still.
  • Colette: She is from France. She has a passion for fashion even about her clothes, her favorite color is pink. Colette is very energetic.
  • Violet: She is from China. She is quiet and likes to read. Violet loves classical music, her favorite instrument is the violin. She does not like to be teased, and has a sensitive nature. When she does not get enough sleep, she can be grouchy.
  • Paulina: She is from Peru. She's shy, sometimes clumsy, but loves to help other rodents. She is good at computers. Paulina loves to travel the world and meet people.
  • Pamela: She is from Tanzania. She is a skilled mechanic, she can fix almost any motor. Pamela loves pizza and she will eat it for breakfast. She is a peacemaker so she doesn't stand arguments.

Ruby Crew[edit]

The Ruby Crew are four mouselets at Mouseford Academy lead by Ruby Flashyfur. They are the rivals of the Thea Sisters.

  • Ruby Flashyfur: Leader of the crew, rival of the Thea sisters and Ryder's sister. She'll do anything to be popular. She has red hair just like her brother, Ryder Flashyfur. In the graphic novels her name is Vanilla de Vissen.
  • Connie: Ruby's second-in command.
  • Alicia: An air-headed member of the Ruby crew. She wears a brown belt and has yellow hair.
  • Zoe: A gothic member of the Ruby crew. She is named after Zoe Saldana and has purple hair.

The Geckos[edit]

A school club for boys.

  • Craig: Craig is the most athletic in the club, and enjoys being the center of attention. He also likes to play music.
  • Shen: He is the shyest rodent in the club. He's sweet, sensitive, and smart. Shen has a crush on one of the Thea Sisters, Pamela.
  • Ryder Flashyfur: Ruby's brother. He has the same red hair as his sister's, but that is where the similarities end. Unlike Ruby, he avoids the spotlight and prefers to keep to himself. In the graphic novels his name is Vic de vissen.

Others[edit]

  • Rebecca Flashyfur: She is Ruby and Ryder's mother. In the Graphic novels her name is Vissia de Vissen.

Characters from Cavemice[edit]

The Stiltonoots[edit]

They are the cavemouse versions of the Stiltons. They lived in a village called Old Mouse City.

  • Geronimo Stiltonoot: He is an ancient ancestor and counterpart of Geronimo Stilton. He runs The Stone Gazette, a prehistoric version of the Rodents Gazette.
  • Thea Stiltonoot: She is an ancient ancestor and counterpart of Thea Stilton. She is Geronimo's younger sister. Thea is a Special reporter for the Stone Gazette.
  • Trap Stiltonoot: He is an ancient ancestor and counterpart of Trap Stilton. He is Geronimo's cousin. Trap likes to play tricks on Geronimo and runs a restaurant called Rotten Tooth Tavern.
  • Benjamin Stiltonoot: He is an ancient ancestor and counterpart of Benjamin Stilton. He is Geronimo's nephew.
  • Grandma Ratrock: She is an ancient ancestor and counterpart of William Shortpaws. She is Geronimo's grandmother.

Recurring characters[edit]

  • Bugsy Wugsy: She is a cavemice version of Geronimo Stilton's Bugsy Wugsy. She is Benjamin's best friend. Despite appearing in the character sheets of every book, she almost never makes a significant appearance.
  • Hercule Poirat: He is a cavemouse version of Geronimo Stilton's Hercule Poirat. He is Geronimo Stiltonoot's best friend.
  • Sally Rockmousen: She is a cavemice version of Sally Ratmousen. She is Geronimo's rival. Sally hosted the Gossip Radio show of Old Mouse City. (Radio stations have not been invented yet; all she does is shout really loud and spread the message).
  • Bluster Conjurat: The city's Shaman.
  • Clarissa Conjurat: The daughter of Bluster and Geronimo's crush.
  • Ernest Heftymouse: The village leader.

Characters from Micekings[edit]

The Stiltonords[edit]

The Stiltonords are the miceking (a play on Viking ) version of the Stiltons. They live in a village called Mouseborg of Miceking Island.

  • Geronimo Stiltonord: He is a mouseking and the Geronimo Stilton from ancient far north. He is the advisor to the miceking's chief.
  • Thea Stiltonord: She is Geronimo's sister. A miceking of Thea Stilton of ancient far north. She is a horse trainer who also works well with all kinds of animals. Thea is also known as The Whisperer because she understands the animals moods and needs.
  • Trap Stiltonord: He is Geronimo's cousin. A mouseking of Trap Stilton of ancient far north. He is a famous inventor of Mouseborg (He is the only inventor in the village). Trap likes to test his inventions on Geronimo.
  • Benjamin Stiltonord: He is Geronimo's nephew. A young mouseking of Benjamin Stilton of ancient far north.

Recurring characters[edit]

  • Bugsilda: She is Benjamin's best friend. A young miceking of Bugsy Wugsy of ancient far north.
  • Sven: Sven is the leader of Mouseborg. They called him The Shouter because he shouts louder than anyone. All of Mouseborg admires and respects him.
  • Thora: She is Sven's daughter. Geronimo has a crush on her.
  • Loki Longsight: the village's fortune teller.

Characters from Spacemice[edit]

The Stiltonix[edit]

The Stiltonix are spacemice version of the Stiltons. They lived in a spaceship called the MouseStar 1.

  • Geronimo Stiltonix: He is a spacemouse and a Geronimo Stilton from a parallel universe. He captains a spaceship called MouseStar 1. He wears a green jumpsuit.
  • Thea Stiltonix: She is Geronimo's sister and a spacemouse version of Thea Stilton. She wears a pink jumpsuit.
  • Trap Stiltonix: He is Geronimo's cousin and a spacemouse version of Trap Stilton. He wears a yellow jumpsuit.
  • Benjamin Stiltonix: He is Geronimo's nephew and a spacemouse version of Benjamin Stilton. He wears a red jumpsuit.
  • William Stiltonix: He is Geronimo's Grandfather and a spacemouse version of William Shortpaws. He wears a dark blue jumpsuit. William was the previous captain of MouseStar 1 before Geronimo.

Recurring characters[edit]

  • Bugsy Wugsy: She is Benjamin's best friend. She is a spacemice version of Geronimo Stilton's Bugsy Wugsy. She wears a lime jumpsuit.
  • Robotix: A multifunction robot. Program to assist on MouseStar 1. Its specialty is intergalactic communication.
  • Professor Greenfur: The scientist of MouseStar 1. He is an expert in alien life-forms.
  • Sally de Wrench: The official mechanic of MouseStar 1. She is excellent at repairing any kinds of machine. Geronimo has a crush on her.
  • Cook Squizzy: MouseStar 1's onboard cook.
  • Hologramix: MouseStar 1 onboard computer.

Character from Creepella von Cacklefur[edit]

The von Cacklefur[edit]

  • Boris von Cacklefur: Creepella's father and funeral director at Fabumouse Funerals, a funeral house. His hobbies are writing romantic poetry and painting graveyard scenes.
  • Shivereen: Creepella's niece. She has a pet chameleon named Moldy and dreams of working in the world of fashion.
  • Grandpa Frankenstein: A mad scientist and an expert on Egyptology.
  • Grandma Crypt: Has a pet tarantula named Dolores
  • Snip and Snap: Troublemaking twins and expert spies. They are good with computers.
  • Baby: Adopted by the Cacklefurs
  • Boneham: The butler of the Cacklefur family.
  • Madame LaTomb: The family housekeeper.
  • Chef Stewrat: The Cacklefur's cook.
  • Booey the Poltergeist: The ghost that haunts the Cacklefur Castle.
  • Chompers The meat-eating guard plant.
  • Kafka: The von Cacklefur family's pet cockroach.

Recurring characters[edit]

  • Billy Squeakspeare: Creepella's new friend. A famous author of romantic novels. He inherited the Squeakspeare mansion belong to his great-great-great-uncle William Squeakspeare.

Ghosts in Squeakspeare Mansion[edit]

  • William Squeakspeare: The previous owner of Squeakspeare Mansion and great-great-great-uncle of Billy Squeakspeare.
  • Simon Snootysnout: The butler. He has centuries of experience of taken care of Squeakspeare Mansion.
  • Bob Woodmouse: The carpenter. He made secret passageways and hidden compartments in furniture.
  • Miss Dustmop: The housekeeper. She is great at mending cobwebs and polishing mold on walls.
  • Hank Hammerat: The blacksmith/locksmith. He makes links of clanking chain for ghost out of any metal.
  • Bonnie Ragu: The cook. She dreams of opening a restaurant for ghosts called The Last Meal.
  • Ted Trimmertail: The Gardener. He masters of making plants wither.
  • Ned Needles: The tailor. He makes stylish spooky fashion for all ghosts in Mysterious Valley.
  • Dreamella Airhead: The maid. She sometimes loses her glasses and finds them in the mansion
  • Gus Sip: The caretaker. He knows all gossips about ghost in Mysterious Valley.
  • Leggy: The spider artist. He makes strong spiderwebs for decorating the mansion.
  • Buzzilla: The toothless mosquito. She loves music and never misses a concert.
  • Arf: The sleepwalking dog. At night, he digs holes in the garden to look for his hidden bones.

The Rattenbaums[edit]

  • The Rattenbaum Triplets: Three sisters that finished each others sentence and has a rivalry with Creepella. Tilly is in green, Milly is in red, and Lilly is in blue.
  • Shamley Rattenbaum: The triplets' grandfather.
  • Lady Fifi: The triplets'grandmother.

Other media[edit]

There are also audio-cassette and CD versions of the Geronimo Stilton books published by Scholastic Audio Books. There are three stories in the first three collections (1–3, 4–6, 7–9), book 10 is sold by itself, and the remaining books are sold in pairs (11–12, 13–14, 15–16, 17–18, 20–21, 22 and 24, 25–26). Books 1 through 10 are read by Edward Herrmann. Books 11–18, 20–22 and 24, and 25–26 are read by Bill Lobley.

TV series[edit]

Main article: Geronimo Stilton (TV series)

An animated series of the same name debuted in Italy on 15 September 2009.[8] The show ran for 78 episodes across three seasons (26 episodes for each season) with the series finale airing on 28 February 2017.

Theatre[edit]

In 2016, Oregon Children's Theatre produced Geronimo Stilton: Mouse in Space, a world-premiere adaptation of Mouse in Space! by John Maclay, and the first live-action production of Geronimo Stilton media in the United States.[9] The production was performed in Canada in 2018.[10]

Musical theatre[edit]

The Kingdom of Fantasy series has been developed into a live show, which has been performed in Spanish in Barcelona, Spain,[11] in Dutch in Hong Kong and The Netherlands,[12] in Dutch/Flemish in Belgium, and in both English and French in Montreal, Canada.[13]

Video games[edit]

Sony Computer Entertainment partnered with Virtual Toys to develop two video games based on the series for PlayStation Portable. The first, Geronimo Stilton in the Kingdom of Fantasy, was released in Europe on 3 November 2011.[14] The follow-up, Geronimo Stilton: Return to the Kingdom of Fantasy, was released in the continent on 31 October 2012.[15] In North America, both titles were released exclusively on the PlayStation Store on 16 July 2013.[16]
